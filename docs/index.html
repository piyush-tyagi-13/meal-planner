Family Usage Guide

üåü Automated Planning

The system automatically selects a unique daily menu (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner). It ensures no meals are repeated from the previous day to keep our menu fresh and exciting.

üìù Managing Recipes

Anyone in the family can contribute to our menu:

  • Browse: Scroll through the "Recipes" tab to see everything available in our cloud.
  • Add New: Tap "+ Add Recipe" to share a new favorite dish.
  • Edit: Tap any recipe card if you want to update ingredients or cooking steps.
  • Categorize: Assign recipes to "Breakfast", "Lunch", or "Dinner" to help the automater pick the right meal for the right time.
